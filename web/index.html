FAQ

Common questions.

What is the managed listing program for vacation property members?
It's for people who own vacation properties through points-based programs (Marriott Vacation Club, Hilton Grand Vacations, Disney Vacation Club, RCI Points, and similar) and don't use all their weeks. We rent your unused inventory through the Vaytoven platform and split the revenue. You don't list, screen guests, or coordinate stays — we do. After you submit the form, a member specialist contacts you within one business day to walk through the specifics for your portfolio.
How is Vaytoven different from Airbnb?
Three things. First, every host is ID-verified before they list — no exceptions. Second, our service fees are roughly half: 14% guest, 3% host versus the incumbents' 14–18% guest plus 14–17% host. Third, payouts release 24 hours after check-in, not seven days later.
When does Vaytoven launch?
We're opening invite-only access in Florida coastal markets (Miami, Naples, Destin) in mid-2026, expanding to Sunbelt markets later that year, and going national by Q1 2027. Sign up for the waitlist to get first access in your region.
What does the platform charge hosts?
A flat 3% host service fee per booking, plus standard Stripe processing (~2.9% + $0.30 on the guest's payment, deducted at the gateway level). No listing fees, no monthly fees, no fee for adding photos or unlisting for a season.
How do you protect against fraud and bad actors?
Identity verification through Stripe Identity at host onboarding. IP and device fingerprinting on every login with anomaly alerts. A real-time fraud rules engine on bookings (new account + high-value + new country triggers manual review). And a dedicated trust team — yes, with humans — who investigate every flag.
Where is my data stored, and what do you log?
Data is stored on AWS in your home region (US users: us-east-1). For security, we log IP, device, and approximate city-level location for every login. We do not collect precise GPS unless you grant it for a specific feature like map search. You can export or delete everything we hold about you from account settings, anytime.
Can I sync my Airbnb or Vrbo calendar?
Yes. We support iCal import and export so your availability stays in sync across platforms. Most hosts set this up in under five minutes.
